## 2. Main Ideas

1. Circadian rhythm = the sun-up/sun-down cycle affecting all human biology
2. The brain has internal clocks (pineal gland + hypothalamus) that sync to this external light/dark/temperature cycle
3. Pre-electric-light humans slept polyphasically — shorter periods more often across 24h
4. Electric light, 24-7 culture, and daylight saving time are the three major disruptions to circadian alignment
5. The R90 goal: build an everyday routine "in harmony with" this natural process

## 3. R90 Principles Extracted

**P-011** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
KSRI = **Key Sleep Recovery Indicator**. There are 7 KSRIs in the R90 system. Circadian rhythm is KSRI 1 — the foundational layer that all other lessons build on.

**P-012** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
The circadian rhythm is a 24-hour biological clock driven by light, dark, and temperature. It is mediated by the **pineal gland** (produces sleep/wake hormones) and the **hypothalamus** (internal clock). These synchronise with external cues.

**P-013** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
Pre-electric-light humans slept in **polyphasic** patterns — biphasic, triphasic, or multiphasic — not a single monophasic block. Monophasic sleep (one block at night) is a modern, post-industrial construct.

**P-014** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
The natural circadian pattern (if living fully outdoors/sunlight) includes:
- Morning: strong light triggers activation
- Midday: natural pause — "fuel and hydrate and have a little chill"
- Afternoon/evening: gradual wind-down as sun diminishes
- Night: sleep state until sunrise restarts the cycle

**P-015** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
Three factors have disrupted modern circadian alignment:
1. **Electric light** (~1700s adoption) → extended wakefulness, suppressed natural cues
2. **24-7 culture / technology** → loss of natural recovery moments between activities
3. **Daylight saving time** → artificial clock shift, seasonal light disruption

**P-016** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
Chronotype is described at end of lesson as "a genetic true reflection of your sleep characteristics." It is innate, not chosen. We "try to camouflage them" but they dominate our everyday. This is the subject of KSRI 2.

## 4. Behavioural Patterns Extracted

**BP-008** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
Modern humans default to **monophasic sleep** (one block at night) — fighting biology. Nick explicitly frames this as a recent aberration, not the natural human pattern.

**BP-009** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
The 24-7 culture eliminated natural "micro-recovery moments" embedded throughout the day — moments that historically occurred when "there was nothing else to do." Technology filled these gaps and removed passive recovery time.

**BP-010** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
People lack awareness of their circadian biology. Nick's prescription for this lesson is simply: search "circadian rhythms," look at the images, understand your relationship with the 24-hour cycle.

## 5. Decision Rules Extracted

**DR-008** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
Build a daily routine that is "in harmony with" the circadian process — aligned to the natural light/dark/temperature cycle rather than imposed by technology or culture.

**DR-009** [CONFIRMED — AUD-001]
